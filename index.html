My St.Louis Life

2013

I moved to St.Louis from sunny South Florida. Some may call me crazy, but all of my wife's family is from STL and I am a great Husband. Our first apartment was in Kirkwood, MO, and the residents included ourselves as well as a cat, Crixus, and a dog, Zuri.

2014

My first job in St.Louis was working for Lifetime Fitness, which was both fun and exciting but very, very, very, very, very, time consuming.

2015

This year marked my transition from Lifetime Fitness to Waterway. An odd jump for sure, but I found out that finding work in St.Louis proved harder than I had ever imagined. I started my career at the Chesterfield location and later that year was transferred to the Woodsmill location.

2016

The definite highlight of 2016 was the birth of my daughters, Isla and Makayla. The first 10 months were the hardest times of my life, with no sleep as well as lots of dirty diapers and crying. After all of that I still would not change a thing.

2017

2017 was highlighted with breaking new ground as not only a first time parent but also as a parent of twins. It also came with great news that we will be having a son in 2018!

2018

Launchcode has been the standout highlight of my life in 2018 as it will hopefully allow me to provide more for my family as well as be able to spend more time with my wife and children. I cannot wait to see what the rest of 2018 brings and am very excited to face any challenges head on!
